# Reflection: Adding Dispute Resolution

A dispute period introduces human oversight to AI‑judged bounties. It makes the system more resilient to AI errors, but adds complexity and delays payout.

**Public vs Hidden:** Answers stay hidden until reveal; disputes are public after they are initiated.

**AI vs Human:** AI handles the initial ranking; the owner (human) resolves disputes, ensuring accountability.

**Trade‑off:** Slightly longer finalization time, but higher trust.
