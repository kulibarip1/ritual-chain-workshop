# DisputableAIBounty – Commit-Reveal with Dispute Period

This contract adds a 24‑hour dispute window after the owner finalizes a winner. Participants can challenge the outcome if they believe the AI judgment was incorrect.

## How it works
1. Commit phase: participants submit hashed answers.
2. Reveal phase: participants reveal their answers.
3. Owner triggers AI judging (off‑chain simulation).
4. Owner finalizes a winner and prize is sent.
5. Any participant can dispute within 24 hours.
6. Owner resolves the dispute by choosing a new winner.
